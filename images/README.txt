KITCHEN BY IQRA — LOGO & PHOTOS
================================

LOGO
----
Put your real logo here as:   logo.png   (a square image works best, e.g. 400x400)

As soon as the file exists at images/logo.png it will appear automatically in the
header of EVERY page (Home, Menu, Office Plans, Blog, Contact and all blog posts).
Until then, the site shows the round "Iq" monogram as a safe fallback — nothing
breaks.

Tip: the logo shows at 50px tall in the header. If your logo file has a lot of
empty white space around it, crop the whitespace tight so the mark looks larger
and sharper. A transparent-background PNG also works and looks cleanest.

DISH PHOTOS
-----------
The food photos currently come from Wikimedia Commons (real, free-to-use photos
of each dish). When you have your own photos of Iqra's actual cooking, you can
replace them: open  js/menu-data.js  and change the `img:` line for any dish to
point at your photo (you can drop the photo in this images/ folder and use, for
example,  img: "images/chicken-biryani.jpg" ). Your real food will always sell
better than stock photos.

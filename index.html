Good to know

Questions people ask us

Do you deliver homemade food near Bahria Town, Lahore?
Yes. We cook in Jubilee Town and deliver daily to Bahria Town, Izmir Town, Tricon Village, Sherawala Heights, Iqbal Avenue and Jubilee Town itself. Delivery charges depend on your exact location.
How do I place an order?
Add dishes to your cart on the menu page and tap "Place order on WhatsApp" — your order opens pre-typed in WhatsApp (0336-4780599). Or simply message us directly.
How much does a homemade lunch cost?
Plates start at PKR 300 (sabzi and karhi pakora) and go up to PKR 600 (white handi, white-sauce pasta). Prices are per plate; delivery is charged separately by location.
Can you handle daily lunch for our whole office?
That's our speciality. Use the weekly office planner to pick a dish and plate count for each day. Delivery is free on 10+ plates per day within our service areas, and you can change any day's dish before 11 AM.
Is the food really homemade?
Yes — everything is cooked the same morning by Iqra in her home kitchen at 252-B Jubilee Town, in small batches, with home-ground masalas. It's ghar ka khana in the most literal sense.
